# CS4381 Game B

## Current Game Rules

### Game Objective

The objective of the game is to be the first team to successfully move all four pieces around the board and completely off the board after returning to the finish/start area.

### Players and Setup

* The game is played by two teams.
* Each team begins with four pieces placed on the Start space.
* To determine which team goes first, one player from each team draws a stick.
* The team with the highest value goes first.

### Turn Structure

Teams alternate turns throughout the game. During a turn, a player throws four sticks. The result determines how many spaces one piece may move.

### Stick Results

* **0** flat sticks (all rounded): move 5 spaces and receive an extra throw.
* **1** flat stick: move 1 space.
* **2** flat sticks: move 2 spaces.
* **3** flat sticks: move 3 spaces.
* **4** flat sticks: move 4 spaces and receive an extra throw.
* If the special stick marked with a red X is the only flat stick showing, move backward 1 space.

### Movement Rules

* Players may move only one piece per throw.
* Pieces must move in the direction of the board.
* A piece must move exactly the number of spaces indicated by the stick throw.
* Shortcut paths exist at specific stations.
* A shortcut is only activated when a piece begins a move from a shortcut station.
* Passing through a shortcut station does not activate the shortcut.

### Shortcut Rules

* Station 5 activates the right-side shortcut path.
* Station 10 activates the left-side shortcut path.
* Station 28 is the center station where shortcut paths meet.
* Movement through the center station depends on the path used to enter it.

### Completion Rules

* Landing on the Start space (station 0) does not complete a lap.
* A piece completes a lap only if it moves past the Start space with movement remaining.
* Once a piece completes a lap, it is removed from the board and considered finished.
* All four pieces must complete a lap for a team to win.

### Red-X Rule

* If the red-X stick result occurs, the selected piece moves backward one station.
* Backward movement follows the path the piece most recently used.
* For stations with multiple possible incoming paths, the previous station determines the correct backward movement.

### Capturing and Combining Pieces

These rules are part of the full game but are deferred for HW2:

* If a piece lands on a space occupied by an opponent's piece, the opponent's piece is sent back to Start.
* If a piece lands on a space occupied by a teammate's piece, those pieces combine and move together as a single piece.
* If a combined piece is captured by an opponent, all pieces in the combined stack are sent back to Start.

### Winning the Game

The first team to successfully move all four pieces around the board and completely off the board wins the game.
